xBomb Wild Multiplier — How Does It Work?

The xBomb Wild is the signature symbol of the Fire in the Hole franchise. In Fire in the Hole 2, the xBomb Wild substitutes for any paying symbol to complete or improve winning combinations. xSplit Symbol

The xSplit symbol is one of the more unique mechanics in Fire in the Hole 2 online slot. If a dirt blocker occupies a position affected by the xSplit, the blocker is removed. Once an xSplit symbol has been used, it converts into a random paying symbol.
